Hola!

Welcome to our website dedicated to saving mangroves, one of the most important ecosystems on our planet. Mangroves are tropical trees and shrubs that grow in coastal areas, including estuaries and lagoons. These unique and diverse forests provide a wide range of ecosystem services, including carbon sequestration, water filtration, and protection against natural disasters such as storm surges and hurricanes.

Problems

Despite their importance, mangroves are rapidly disappearing due to human activities such as deforestation, aquaculture, and urbanization. In fact, it is estimated that half of the world's mangrove forests have been lost over the past century. This is a concerning trend, as the loss of mangroves can have devastating consequences for local communities and the environment.

It's not over yet

However, there is hope. Through conservation efforts, we can protect and restore mangrove forests. By working together to raise awareness and take action, we can ensure that these critical ecosystems continue to thrive for generations to come. On this website, you will find information about the importance of mangroves, the threats they face, and what you can do to help. Together, we can make a difference and protect these invaluable ecosystems.
